# Project Details

## Project Title: KeyShare

## Problem Statement:
Accessing a vehicle as and when required may not always be efficient, cost-effective, or even convenient. There are a number of people and companies who own cars that are available but not being used all the time, and there are also a number of people who are not able to access a vehicle for a short time.

## Proposed Solution:
KeyShare is a platform that allows people and companies to list available cars for rent, enabling them to search and compare cars for rental whenever they need them. The purpose of KeyShare is to make it easier for people to access cars by offering a simple system for listing cars, checking availability, and renting them.

## Project Scope:
- **Aim:**  
 To create a functional working application that would provide users with a seamless way to find, list, and rent vehicles, helping to solve the problem of inefficient vehicle access.

- **Objectives:**
1. Develop a system that enables individuals or businesses to list their cars that are available for rent.
2. Develop a system that enables users to easily search and view available vehicles.
3. Provide adequate information on vehicles, including model, price, location, etc.
4. Ensure that the rental process is simple, fast, and convenient.

## Application Description:
KeyShare is an application designed for vehicle rental and sharing. This application is intended for two types of users: vehicle owners/companies and renters. Vehicle owners/companies are allowed to list their vehicles, while renters are allowed to select the vehicle they want according to their needs. This application is designed to be user-friendly, accessible, and convenient.

### Main Users:
- Individuals who wish to rent a car for temporary use.
- Businesses that wish to rent out their vehicles.
- Car owners who wish to make money by renting out unused cars.

### Key Features:
- User registration and login
- Vehicle listing and management
- Search and filter available cars
- Vehicle details page
- Booking/rental request system
- Availability tracking
- Simple and user-friendly interface
